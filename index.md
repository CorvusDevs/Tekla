# Tekla

**Virtual Keyboard for macOS with Swipe-to-Type**

Tekla is a full virtual keyboard for macOS featuring swipe-to-type, smart word predictions, and support for 14 languages. Everything runs 100% offline on your Mac.

- Swipe-to-type with intelligent word recognition
- Smart word predictions and next-word suggestions
- 14 languages: English, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Turkish, Polish, Russian
- Full desktop keyboard layout with function keys
- 100% offline — no data leaves your Mac
- Lightweight and energy-efficient
